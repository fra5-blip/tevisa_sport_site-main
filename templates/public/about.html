About TEVISA

The Technical Education and Vocational Institutions Sports Association (TEVISA) is a sports association dedicated to fostering athletic development and competition among technical and vocational institutions across Zambia. TEVISA serves as a platform for students to showcase their sporting talents, build teamwork, and promote physical fitness, while instilling discipline and the values of fair play. Our Mission Our mission is to promote sports participation and physical education among technical and vocational students in Zambia, providing opportunities for personal growth, physical fitness, and the development of sporting talent. Our Vision TEVISA envisions a future where every student in Zambia's technical and vocational institutions has access to quality sports programs and opportunities to compete in national and international competitions.

Objectives

To organize and manage annual sports tournaments for technical and vocational institutions across Zambia. To provide a platform for students to develop and showcase their talents in various sports. To promote the spirit of competition, teamwork, and fair play among students. To enhance collaboration between institutions through sports. To contribute to the overall health and well-being of students through regular physical activities.

TEVISA Activities

TEVISA organizes a range of sporting activities and tournaments every year, bringing together students from technical and vocational institutions across the country. Our activities include: Annual Sports Tournaments We host a wide variety of sports competitions, including:

  • Football (Soccer)
  • Basketball
  • Volleyball
  • Netball
  • Athletics (Track and Field)
  • Chess
  • Tennis

These tournaments are held at both regional and national levels, and participants have the opportunity to compete for honors, scholarships, and recognition from the sports community in Zambia. Workshops & Training Programs In addition to competitions, TEVISA organizes workshops for coaches, sports officials, and students, focusing on:

  • Sports management
  • Coaching and training techniques
  • Sportsmanship and fair play
  • Physical fitness and health education

TEVISA Member Institutions

TEVISA membership is open to all technical and vocational institutions in Zambia. Member institutions actively participate in TEVISA-organized tournaments and events, benefiting from our sports programs, coaching workshops, and training seminars. Some of our esteemed member institutions include:

  • Institute of Business and Industrial Practice (ZIBSIP)
  • Evelyn Hone College
  • Nkrumah University
  • Northern Technical College (NORTEC)
  • Livingstone Institute of Business and Engineering Studies (LIBES)
And many more...

Why Join TEVISA?

Opportunities for Competition:

  • TEVISA provides a structured platform for your institution to compete at regional and national levels in various sports.
  • Talent Development: Students have a chance to grow and excel in their chosen sports disciplines.
  • Networking: Connect with other institutions, coaches, and athletes across Zambia.
  • Access to Resources: Gain access to workshops, training programs, and sporting events that help develop students both physically and mentally.
  • National Recognition: Outstanding performers in TEVISA tournaments often receive recognition and opportunities at national sports events.
