Students are free to register in a sports discipline of interest with the patron. Currently the available sports disciplines are Netball, Chess, Football, Volleyball and Basketball. Students travel to different colleges in Zambia to take part in tournaments like TEVISA games, Heroes & unity games, independence Games, ZAFUCCS games. Additionally, students also participate in international tournaments in neighboring countries under SADC like Namibia, Botswana & Swaziland.
